Vocab Quiz Hangman

Rules

  • You start with 100 HP (grade points).
  • Each wrong guess deducts 5 HP.
  • If you fail to guess a word in three tries, an additional 10 HP is deducted.
  • Switching tabs during the game deducts 50 HP (once per game).
  • The game has 20 levels, one for each vocab word.
  • Your final grade is your remaining HP after all levels.
